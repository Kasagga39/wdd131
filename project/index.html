About MJ Electronics & Energies

Description: MJ Electronics and Energies is a locally owned and operated company based in Masaka City, Uganda. We specialize in providing cutting-edge, sustainable energy solutions and advanced electronic systems to meet the needs of residential, commercial, and institutional customers in the region.

Our Mission

To empower Masaka and surrounding communities by delivering reliable, sustainable energy and electronics solutions that improve quality of life, boost productivity, and foster safety — while reducing dependency on unstable grid power and minimizing environmental impact.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of clean energy and intelligent electronic solutions in Central Uganda, known for quality, innovation, and social responsibility.
