Country/Place Page – Site Plan Document

Site Name

Name: Explore Uganda

Description: "Explore Uganda". The name reflects the site's goal of inviting visitors to discover and appreciate the city’s historical significance and hidden gems.

Optional Domain: exploreuganda.jp

Site Purpose

The purpose of "Explore Uganda" is to provide a central resource for travelers, students, and culture enthusiasts interested in Kyoto. The site offers detailed information about attractions, cultural practices, local cuisine, and travel tips. Users can also find recommended itineraries and real-time seasonal highlights.

Scenarios

  • What are the top must-see temples and shrines in Uganda?
  • Which local dishes should I try when visiting Uganda?
  • When is the best time of year to visit Uganda for cherry blossoms?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Navy Blue (#003366) – used for headers and navigation background.

Accent Color: Vermilion Orange (#ff6600) – used for buttons, links, and call-to-action elements.

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Merriweather', serif – used for main titles and section headers.
  • Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif – used for paragraphs and general content.
  • Special Sections: 'Noto Sans JP', sans-serif – used for Japanese text or cultural notes.
